How It Works

We bridge the gap between your business and licensed payment infrastructure

CorridorBridge does not move money. We help you choose the right partners, build the controls, and design the workflows that make every cross-border transaction defensible.

01

Assess Your Readiness

We review your current payment processes, controls, documentation, and partner relationships to identify gaps, risks, and compliance exposures.

02

Design Your Controls

We design governance frameworks, approval workflows, segregation of duties, and vendor risk procedures tailored to your corridor and regulatory obligations.

03

Operate with Confidence

Your payments move through properly-vetted licensed partners, supported by documented workflows, audit-ready evidence, and ongoing advisory support.

Our Services

Eight ways we help you move money safely across borders

From initial readiness assessments to ongoing executive advisory — every engagement is built around your corridor, your regulatory obligations, and your operational reality.

Payment Readiness Assessment

We evaluate your current processes, controls, and documentation to identify compliance gaps, risk exposures, and operational weaknesses. Deliverable: readiness report, gap analysis, and 90-day remediation plan.

Payment Partner Selection

We help you identify and evaluate suitable licensed providers — banks, gateways, MSBs, and fintech rails — based on your corridor, transaction type, and regulatory profile. Deliverable: partner scorecard and selection memo.

Payment Workflow Design

We design your end-to-end payment process — from invoice to approval to initiation to reconciliation. Structured, auditable, and scalable across all your corridors.

Governance & Controls Advisory

We establish the governance framework required for compliant operations: approval matrices, segregation of duties, access controls, oversight procedures, and risk ownership structures.

Vendor & Third-Party Risk Review

We evaluate the operational and compliance risks in your partner relationships — fintechs, banks, processors, and gateways — before you commit or renew.

Fintech Market Readiness

For fintechs expanding across borders — from Africa into Canada or globally — we help you strengthen governance, operating models, partner readiness, and enterprise credibility.

Cybersecurity & Operational Risk

We review control environments, access management, incident response, resilience, and security governance connected to your cross-border payment operations.

Executive & Strategic Advisory

Leadership-level advisory for founders and executives navigating cross-border payment strategy, partner selection, risk considerations, and operational direction. Available as fractional or retained engagement.

About CorridorBridge

We are the trusted bridge between businesses and the licensed payment ecosystem

CorridorBridge Advisory Inc. was established to serve as an independent advisory firm for organizations navigating the complexity of cross-border payment operations between Africa and North America.

We do not replace banks, payment service providers, or licensed fintech operators. Instead, we help clients choose the right licensed partners, design strong internal controls, reduce operational and compliance risk, and build well-structured payment workflows that support safe and reliable cross-border transactions.
